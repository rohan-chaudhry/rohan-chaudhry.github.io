about me

I am a senior mechanical engineering student minoring in computer science at the University of Texas at Austin, and I am interested in pursuing a full-time career in technical consulting, software engineering, and data analysis.

Currently, I am working alongside a civil engineering professor at UT to analyze campus water and enery consumption. You can learn about the project below, but feel free to reach out if you would like any additional info.

I am also working with argodesign, an Austin-based design firm, through an Integrated Technology & Design class, in which I'm learning how to apply engineering principals in designing a product that focuses on the user experience and involves a software aspect.

In the past, I've worked for Schneider Electric in the north Houston area in their buildings automated systems department, created custom 3D-printed trophies for student org events alongside my engineering design professor, and served as the Director for the Society of Freshman Engineers on campus.

Thanks for visiting my site! If you're interested in learning more about what I have done, you can check out some of my favorites below and some more on my Github. Happy browsing!
